Experience

  • Since June'24, I have been working closely with the team at SketchX lab on a creative idea inspired by NVIDIA's Instant-ngp in the domain of 3D Computer Vision aimed at a publication.
  • From May'24-July'24, I was an on-site research intern at Dr. Swagatam Das's Machine Learning Research Lab at ISI Kolkata. My work there focused on Graph Structure Learning for mitigation of oversmoothing in Deep-GNNs, aimed at a short publication.
  • Since May'24, I am collaborating with Dr. Amitava Das's Team at AIISC on a publication focused on developing a first-of-its-kind dataset based on classification and identification of hallucinations in AI-Generated Text-to-Videos. My contributions include developing the pipeline for benchmarking and classification of hallucinations in AI-generated videos, along with inferencing the current state-of-the-art models in the Text2Video domains.
  • I worked as an AI research intern at MirrAR from Dec'24 - Mar'24, where I worked with Dr. Sameer's Team on a 3D Hand Mesh Reconstruction pipeline. My work there broadly dealt with Stacked Hourglass Networks, GCNs, Spiral Convolutions, and Heatmaps for joint estimation.
  • During early 2024, I was briefly involved with PostDoc Xingjian Li of Xu Lab@CMU on employing NeuralODEs for Image Segmentation of CryoET Data.
  • My team was the Winner of the ML Track of the 24-hour-long Hackathon Silicon Maze'23, conducted by NIT-K(National Institute of Technology, Surathkal).
