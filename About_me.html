About me

I am currently a sophomore at the esteemed Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi, pursuing a (B.Tech + M.Tech) dual degree in Mathematics and Computing. The prospect of delving into the intricate relationship between mathematics and technology is both fascinating and intellectually stimulating, and my undergraduate coursework provides an ideal foundation for such exploration.

During my academic journey, I have dedicated a substantial amount of time to mastering machine learning, seamlessly integrating deep learning into my coursework whenever possible. While I acknowledge that my exposure to deep learning is relatively brief, my current aspiration is to evolve into a Research Scientist specializing in this field.My research interests span the broader domains of deep learning, with a specific focus on Vision-Language models, Representation Learning, and the intersection of Neuroscience and AI. However, I am not confined to these areas and actively seek opportunities to cultivate a research mindset in the expansive field of deep learning.

Beyond my deep learning pursuits, I have cultivated a keen interest in quantum computing. I have invested significant time in comprehending the fundamentals through various courses and have developed an enduring fascination for Quantum Algorithms and Quantum Machine Learning. Notably, I hold certifications from QWorld in Quantum Key Distribution (QKD), Quantum Algorithms, among others.

Looking ahead, I am poised to contribute meaningfully to the evolving landscape of both deep learning and quantum computing, driven by a commitment to rigorous research and an insatiable curiosity for innovation
